1. Introduction

Wordle VS ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using Wordle VS, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Username or display name
  • Profile image (if provided)
  • Apple Sign In credentials (as provided by Apple)

If you use Sign In with Apple, we receive only the information Apple shares with us.

Gameplay Information

We collect information necessary to operate multiplayer features, including:

  • Match history
  • Game moves and results
  • Win/loss records
  • Reactions sent during matches
  • Timestamps of gameplay activity

This information is used solely to operate the game and display match results.

Technical & Usage Data

We may collect limited technical information such as:

  • Device type
  • App version
  • Crash logs
  • Performance metrics
  • Push notification tokens

This data helps us maintain app stability and improve performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Operate multiplayer matches
  • Display usernames and profile images to other players
  • Send match-related push notifications
  • Improve gameplay experience
  • Monitor performance and fix bugs
  • Prevent abuse or misuse

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Multiplayer Visibility

When playing a match, other players may see:

  • Your username
  • Your profile image
  • Match history with them
  • Reactions sent during gameplay

This visibility is limited to in-app interactions.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate the app, including:

  • Supabase (database and backend services)
  • Apple Sign In
  • Push notification services
  • Analytics providers

These providers may process data on our behalf according to their own privacy policies.

6. Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, we store a device push notification token to send:

  • Turn reminders
  • Match updates
  • Game-related alerts

You may disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

7. Data Storage & Security

User data is stored using secure cloud infrastructure providers.

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data, but no system can guarantee complete security.

8. Data Retention

We retain account and gameplay data as long as your account remains active.

You may request deletion of your account at any time.

9. Account Deletion

You may delete your account directly within the app (Settings → Delete Account) or by contacting:

wordlevs@gmail.com

10. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.
